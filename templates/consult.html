Your health deserves expert care and personal attention

Why Consultation Is Crucial

While our Brain Tumor Classifier can help detect anomalies in MRI scans, it cannot replace a qualified neurologist, oncologist, or radiologist. Professional medical evaluation is essential for accurate diagnosis, treatment planning, and ongoing care.

When to See a Doctor

  • If you are experiencing symptoms such as persistent headaches, vision issues, or seizures
  • If the classifier detects a potential tumor in your MRI
  • If you have a family history of brain tumors or related neurological disorders
  • After receiving a new MRI scan or unusual report findings

How to Prepare for Your Appointment

  • Gather all MRI scans, reports, and notes
  • Document your symptoms, their frequency, and intensity
  • Write down any questions or concerns in advance
  • Bring someone you trust for support, if possible
